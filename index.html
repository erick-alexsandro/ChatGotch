Chat-Gotch - Help

By the way, "Chat" in Chat-Gotch means "Cat" in french and, of course, "chat". And "Gotch" comes from the name of the game Tamagotchi.

Commands

  • /help
  • /findall
  • /findpet
  • /addpet
  • /editpet
  • /deletepet
  • /editimage
  • /devour
  • /fight
/help

This command brought you here! :)

  • Command Options
  • There's no options for this command.
/findall

This command is used to find all pets.

  • Command Options
  • There's no options for this command.
/findpet

Search for a specific pet.

  • Command Options
  • /findpet [nameofpet] - Name of the pet to search for.
/addpet

Add a new pet.

  • Command Options
  • /addpet [nameofpet] - Name of your pet.
  • /addpet [datebirth] - Date of your pet's birth.
  • /addpet [father] - Name of your pet's father.
  • /addpet [image] - Attachment to your pet's image.
/editpet

Edit a pet. (You must be the parent of the pet)

Note: Those are the items you can change: father, mother, name.

  • Command Options
  • /editpet [nameofpet] - Name of the pet to edit.
  • /editpet [item] - What on your pet would you like to edit.
  • /editpet [newvalue] - The new value of the item you want to edit.
/deletepet

Delete a pet. (You must be the parent of the pet)

  • Command Options
  • /deletepet [nameofpet] - Name of the pet to delete.
/editimage

Edit image of a pet. (You must be the parent of the pet)

  • Command Options
  • /editimage [nameofpet] - Name of the pet to edit image.
  • /editimage [image] - Attachment with your new pet's image.
/devour

Makes your pet devour another pet. (You must be the parent of the pet)

The devoured pet receives +1 of his life count.

The victim must already be dead and not devoured.

  • Command Options
  • /devour [nameofpet] - Name of your pet.
  • /devour [victim] - Name of the pet you want to devour.
/fight

Make your pet fight another pet. (You must be the parent of the pet)

It randomly chooses one of the pets to be the winner. The winner receives +1 to their life count. While the loser loses 1 life count.

One the opponent's parents must accept the challenge to fight. If not accepted within 3 minutes, the fight is declined automatically.

  • Command Options
  • /fight [nameofpet] - Name of your pet.
  • /fight [opponent] - Name of the pet you want to fight.
